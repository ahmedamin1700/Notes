# Measures of spread and variability

## Range

The difference between the samllest variable and the largest.

## IQR = Interquartile

IQR = 3rd quartile - 1st quartile.

## Variance

The average of the distances from the mean squared.

```latex
variance = \frac{ Σ(x - μ)^2}{n}
```
